I’m Lucas Luxton.

This site is my personal space to publish work in public: photographs, essays, and notes on whatever I’m learning. It’s intentionally broad. If it’s interesting to me, it belongs here.

## What I publish

### Photography

Photo sets, single images, and small visual essays—mostly street, architecture, transit, and the in-between moments that make a place feel real.

### Writing

Posts on:

- Tech and products
- Business, sales, and leadership
- AI (what’s useful, what’s changing, what’s noise)
- Anything else worth capturing while it’s still fresh
## Gear

- Leica Q2 (28mm f/1.7 Summilux)

- Leica M10 with 50mm f/1.4 Summilux-M

- Canon R5 with RF 24–70mm f/2.8

## Why this exists
  
I wanted one place that isn’t optimized for algorithms—a simple archive I control. A home for photos, a place to think in writing, and a running log of what I’m paying attention to.
